## Components

### Button

- Padding: 12px 24px
- Font size: 14px, weight 600
- Radius: 8px
- States: default, hover (darken 10%), active (darken 20%), disabled (opacity 50%)

### Card

- Padding: 24px
- Radius: 12px
- Shadow: md
- Border: 1px neutral-200

### Input

- Padding: 12px 16px
- Border: 1px neutral-300
- Focus ring: 2px primary at 50% opacity
- Radius: 8px

### Nav

- Height: 64px
- Link padding: 8px 16px
- Mobile below 768px: drawer or sticky-collapse

### Section

- Vertical rhythm: 32px between major sections
- Max-width: 1280px (desktop)
- Padding: 32px horizontal on tablet, 24px on mobile

## Motion

- **Fast**: 150ms
- **Base**: 250ms
- **Slow**: 400ms
- **Easing**: ease-in-out (standard), ease-out (entrances), ease-in (exits)

## Breakpoints

- sm: 640px
- md: 768px
- lg: 1024px
- xl: 1280px

## Density Mode

*comfort | compact | airy*

- Chosen: **comfortable**
