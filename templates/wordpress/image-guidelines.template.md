## Per-Image Prompt Structure

*Use this checklist to construct prompts for stage 5 (`05-image-prompts.md`):*

- Subject: what/who is in the frame?
- Setting: context, location, environment?
- Lighting: time of day, direction, quality?
- Mood: emotional tone, energy level?
- Dimensions: exact pixels and aspect ratio
- Style cue: photography style, post-processing, reference?
- Negative cue: what to avoid in the image?
